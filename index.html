    To Get Lost

    From remote wildernesses to hectic metropolises, often one of the greatest experiences to have whilst travelling is to lose all sense of time and direction and get thoroughly caught up in your new surroundings.Here’s list of some place to get lost.

  • Wandering the souqs of Marrakech, Morocco : The bustling outdoor markets of Marrakech are an exercise for all of your senses; noises from every direction, vibrant colours, and spices scent the air. Perfect for a wander and for losing any sense of direction!
  • The Turkish city of Istanbul : where East so famously meets West is also home to numerous historical and cultural gems. The Grand Bazaar is one such; a labyrinthine indoor market wonderfully impossible to navigate your way around.
